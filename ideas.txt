Let castles continue to develop after the player died and got eradicated from his throne.

Castle zoom in:
You can place roads, structures, etc. in build mode. Roads are cosmetic, but other structures serve a purpose.
Some units go around the town but its only cosmetic in the client.
New build stuff is sent to the server to have it saved between sessions.
The town data is only sent to the client when he is in town view (TownState).
